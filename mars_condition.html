Quick facts about Mars

Surface Facts

  • Ground is rich in Iron Oxide
  • Surface is rocky with canyons, volcanoes, dry lake beds and craters
  • Surface temperature varies from -145 C to 35 C
  • Atomosphere is thin with barely any Oxygen
  • Power source: 350 watts
  • Rotor-speed: 2400 rpm
  • Maximum altitude: 10 m
  • Maximum speed: 10 m/s
